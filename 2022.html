2022 Champion:
University of Texas

The University of Texas Longhorns claimed the 2022 NCAA Women's Volleyball National Championship, ending a decade long pursuit of the program's third NCAA national title. The Longhorns' triumphant return to the top was fueled by the exceptional leadership of Logan Eggleston, the first Texas player to be named National Player of the Year.

Head Coach Jerritt Elliott guided the team to this decisive victory, earning his second national championship. In his 23rd year leading the program, Coach Elliott's was able to lead the Longhorns in returning the to the pinnacle of collegiate volleyball.
