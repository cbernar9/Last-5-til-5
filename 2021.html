2021 Champion:
University of Wisconsin

The University of Wisconsin secured the 2021 NCAA Women's Volleyball National Championship, capturing the program's first national title in its history. This historic victory followed multiple tournament appearances and was powered by National Player of the Year Dana Rettke.

The team was led by Head Coach Kelly Sheffield, who in his 12th year at the helm of the program, guided the Badgers to their long-awaited championship breakthrough.
